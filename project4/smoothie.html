What Is A Berry Broz Smoothie?

This smoothie contains berries that serve a purpose in providing the proper nutrition our bodies need in order to start the day to its fullest. Each berry has its own discrete quality, but still keeps that brotherly bond that plays a part in balancing the body of the drinker; while at the same time providing a sweet taste. Combined with a colorful pigment, this drink is perfect for breakfast and an afternoon snack. In addition, the beverage in itself takes about five minutes to create. Ingredients

    • blueberries
